Els-Bels Boutique is run by Oranmore mum Eleanor, who realised that her love of sewing, nurtured and developed during Covid lockdown, could give pleasure to people other than just her own household. Her attention to detail, and love of pairing beautiful fabrics and hardware, mean that her bags and accessories are always a special addition to any outfit. And if you're searching for the perfect bag to match an outfit for a special occasion, Eleanor delights in working with customers to source fabrics, patterns and hardware to be able to produce just the bag they're looking for.
